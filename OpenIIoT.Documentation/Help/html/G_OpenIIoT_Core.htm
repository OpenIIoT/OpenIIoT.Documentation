OpenIIoT.Core.Model
The Model namespace encapsulates the model for the application. The model consists of two collections of Items; a parent/child tree of Items that implements the composite design pattern, and a dictionary keyed on the FQN of Items and containing a reference to the keyed Item. The Item composite allows for logical management and retrieval of data from the model, while the dictionary provides fast lookups of model items.
